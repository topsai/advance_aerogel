OUR TECHNOLOGY

HONG HITECH developed a totally new technology to manufacture aerogel material. Traditionally aerogel is made from silica gel through a process called supercritical where silica gel with solvent go through high pressure and high temperature to evaporate the solvent and keep the structure of the gel not to collapse. Currently most of the silica aerogel are made through this process, which is more expensive and slow.




Our Hong technology is totally different. We use a worldwide patented technology called phased decompression to manufacture silica aerogel. Instead of high pressure and high temperature, we developed our unique IP technology called phased decompression. We go the opposite of high temperature and high pressure by lowering the pressure through phases to draw the solvent out and maintain the structure not to collapse at the same time. This technology is patented in US, Europe, Japan and China.




We Can Help You

We can produce high quality aerogel fast and economically. Our aerogel is very high quality with light weight, and big surface to weight.

At the meantime our cost is low and can transfer the saving to our customers.

2. Aerogel insulating blanket:


Aerogel blanket are fiber mesh sheets coated with aerogel liquid. It has the best thermal insulation property and makes the ideal insulation wrapping for oil industry, the cryo industry (such as LNG shipping, piping), the power industry, the building industry etc.





Hong Hitech has developed another patented technology to separate our aerogel sheet from others. Traditionally, the coating of the aerogel onto fiber mesh is done by submerge the fabric into the coating liquid, then take out and heated to dry. The product made by this process has some major quality issue. As the blanket bend or with the rain and shine, the coating will peel out, then of course the thermal protection will decrease. You may need to add or replace the defect blanket.

Our technology using high vacuum pressure to suck the aerogel into the fiber sheet and mixed with the fiber. This made our product so much different from others as they almost will not peeling off and resist to whether beaten, so eliminate the chance to add or replace blanket cost. The customers could enjoy longer thermal insulating time and cost saving.
