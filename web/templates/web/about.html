Aerogel is one of the best new material in the world, which is developed by NASA and applied to commercial use in last couple of decades.

Aerogels are a diverse class of ultralow density solids that combine multiple disparate and extreme materials properties into a single material envelope. Aerogel materials generally exhibit a high degree of porosity, high specific surface area, and superlative energy damping (thermal, acoustic, and impact) properties.

Silica aerogels are a common type of aerogel that combine ultralow thermal conductivity (as low as 10 mW m-1 K-1) with extremely low density (as low as 0.001 g cm-3), high specific surface area (500-2000 m2 g-1), low dielectric constant (as low as 1.02), and good optical transparency (~91% per cm), making them uniquely valuable as high-performance insulators, supports for chemical sensors and catalysts, and particle detectors.

2. Aerogel insulating blanket:


Aerogel blanket are fiber mesh sheets coated with aerogel liquid. It has the best thermal insulation property and makes the ideal insulation wrapping for oil industry, the cryo industry (such as LNG shipping, piping), the power industry, the building industry etc.





Hong Hitech has developed another patented technology to separate our aerogel sheet from others. Traditionally, the coating of the aerogel onto fiber mesh is done by submerge the fabric into the coating liquid, then take out and heated to dry. The product made by this process has some major quality issue. As the blanket bend or with the rain and shine, the coating will peel out, then of course the thermal protection will decrease. You may need to add or replace the defect blanket.

Our technology using high vacuum pressure to suck the aerogel into the fiber sheet and mixed with the fiber. This made our product so much different from others as they almost will not peeling off and resist to whether beaten, so eliminate the chance to add or replace blanket cost. The customers could enjoy longer thermal insulating time and cost saving.
