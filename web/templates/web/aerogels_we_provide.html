The insulation felt series produced by Hongna Luyuan company of our company is another special technology developed by Beijing Hongda Polytron Technologies Inc, making our aerogel insulation blanket much better than other similar products. Traditionally, aerogels are applied to the fiber net by immersing the fabric in the coating solution. Then remove and heat to dry. There are some major quality problems in the products made by this process: the coating will peel off as the blanket is bent or under rain and sunlight, resulting in the decrease of thermal protection effect, and the aerogel insulation blanket needs to be added or replaced.
Our technology uses vacuum negative pressure to absorb aerogels into fiber chips and mix with fibers. This makes our products very different from other products because they hardly peel off and are not easy to age. Therefore, customers can enjoy longer insulation time and save cost without frequent addition or replacement of products.
