宏纳绿源公司介绍



北京弘纳绿源科技发展有限公司成立于2017年，是一家以绿色环保技术研发、环保产品生产与服务、环保工程咨询与实施为主的高科技企业，公司秉持人与自然和谐相处的理念，始终倡导绿色、生态的环保新导向。公司核心高科技产品是植物源无毒杀菌除臭剂，其技术由公司董事长历经十余年持续研发改进，具有自主知识产权，产品可高效除臭、除异味；能消毒杀菌、能杀虫灭卵、能驱蚊灭蝇、无毒、无残留、无公害。该产品集杀虫杀菌为一体，施用方便快捷，广泛适用于绿色种植及养殖、卫生消杀等方面，尤其适用于城市环卫垃圾处理和城市园林绿化。

We are the exclusive distributor of Hong Hitech Co. (Beijing) Ltd. and Hongna luyuan Technology Development Co. Ltd. In North America region.

Founded in 2017, Beijing Hongna Luyuan Technology Development Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise focusing on environmental technology research and development, environmental protect production and service, environmental engineering consulting and implementation. The company adheres to the concept of harmony between human-being and nature and advocate green and ecological new environmental protection. The company's core high-tech products are plant-derived non-toxic germicidal deodorants. Its technology has been continuously researched and improved by the company's chairman for more than ten years. It has independent intellectual property rights, and its products can deodorize and remove odors efficiently; it can be disinfected, eliminate insects eggs, repel mosquitoes, fly, non-toxic, no residue, no pollution. The product is convenient and quick to apply. It can be widely used in planting and breeding, sanitation, etc. It is especially suitable for urban sanitation garbage disposal and urban gardening.
